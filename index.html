SKILLS

User Research

My career so far has revolved around creating impactful customer-facing collateral. Doing this successfully has only possible through thorough research online and user research methods like A/B testing and survey forms. Recently I have also begun to experiment with interviews derived from in depth UX research plans, contextual inquiries, card sorting, and usability testing.

User Empathy

Traditionally, I have created user personas based on industry research and insight about our real customers, gathered from the sales teams in order to get into the mindset of our real customers. Using methods like user personas and empathy maps have allowed me to tap into needs, wants, and motivations of our users so that I can alleviate their frustrations through the proper Information architecture.
